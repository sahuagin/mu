## Key architectural decisions

1. **DynamicViewport over ratatui Terminal** — ratatui's Viewport::Inline is fixed-height at creation. Recreating Terminal leaves ghost frames. Custom viewport uses raw DECSTBM for grow (scroll above up) and Pi-style shrink (keep y, blank below).

2. **Full repaint over diff** — After many insert_before calls, buffer state gets confused. Full repaint (write all cells every frame) is correct and negligible overhead on local terminal. Diff can be restored later with proper invalidation (bead mu-sy03).

3. **snap_to_bottom after send** — Prevents streaming insert_before calls from triggering push-downs (which create blank line gaps). Viewport is always at screen bottom when streaming starts.

4. **Pi-style shrink** — On prompt clear/shrink, viewport.y stays the same (doesn't move down). Blank space goes below. Conversation above stays undisturbed. Observed in pi_agent_rust.

5. **Codex patterns studied** — Their custom_terminal.rs uses: scroll regions for history insertion (\\r\\n within a region), synchronized output brackets, ClearToEnd per row, visible_history_rows tracking. Filed as beads for future implementation.
